Ingredients

  • 8 cups (1920 mL) apple cider
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 6 cardamom pods, cracked
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 whole cloves
  • Â½ cup (50 g) fresh cranberries
  • 1 Tbsp (6 g) fresh ginger, thinly sliced

    • Mullied Spice Apple Cider

    This mulled cider looks beautifully festive with the bright orange and red fruits.

    The aroma of apple and cinnamon simmering away on the stove will make your home smell absolutely incredible. A drink that serves both as an air freshener and a deliciously sweet warm drink? Cheers to that!

    Directions

    1. Place all ingredients in a large pot and bring to a boil over high heat.

    2.Reduce heat to a simmer, partially cover, and let the flavours infuse for 20 - 30 minutes.

    3.Serve warm. Enjoy!

    STORAGE

    Best to enjoy immediately.

    Can be made ahead of time and reheated on the stove over medium heat until warmed through. Store in the fridge for up to 1 day.
